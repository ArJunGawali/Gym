Who we Are & What We Do

ABOUT US

We pride ourselves on creative and professional route-setting, a friendly and knowledgeable staff composed entirely of climbers, the best in new and fresh holds and volumes, and being the home base for an amazing community!

We promotes physical activity, provides a safe, functional, and comfortable workout environment, and creates a pleasant and enjoyable atmosphere for recreation and socialization.

Our many amenities such as yoga classes, climbing clinics, weights and cardio equipment, saunas, and shower facilities mean members can make the gym a big part of their lives.

We promote responsible enjoyment of outdoor climbing areas and Leave-No-Trace principles. We are committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming environment to train, learn, and develop a passion for climbing!
